  • Hi, I'm Sophie Ho  👋

    I’m a journalist with experience mapping audience/user needs to editorial decisions and product strategy. Most recently, I was a product strategist at Google News, focusing on news ranking and quality, after five years at The Washington Post as its News Analytics editor.

    Throughout my career, I’ve been driven by the dual missions of helping everyone have access to high quality news, and helping publishers become sustainable through understanding their audiences’ needs. I’ve been able to work on these challenges in many different capacities over the years by leveraging my skills in audience development, data analytics, product strategy, and user experience research in both newsrooms and product tech companies.

    I’m currently on the hunt for something new, hoping to bring my mix of skills to the right team. Welcome!

    What I’ve done:

    Grown audience and traffic for The Washington Post, first as an engagement editor and later as the News Analytics editor.

    Led strategy for newsroom products, building and delivering tools to help journalists understand their audiences and subscribers.

    Spearheaded editorial and SEO strategies to increase reach of breaking news coverage to reach new audiences.

    Experimented with new media and formats, such as an artificial intelligence bot that wrote hundreds of stories and a messenger bot that asked how readers felt about the 2016 election (that one got an award!).

    Conducted comprehensive content research, reading and analyzing 89,052 Washington Post stories to recommend strategies to engage with new audiences.

    What I’m looking to do more of:

    Community projects with the wider journalism community, especially with people of color and women.Some projects I’ve done with the journalism community include collaborating with 100+ journalists to build the DEI Coalition For Anti-Racist, Equitable, And Just Newsrooms, a digital community for journalists who want to work toward a better future. As one of four committee leads, I facilitated a group who crafted our community’s agreements and effective response framework.

    Teaching and speaking on my favorite subjects. Past talks I’ve done include: How to make spaces you're in more inclusive, how to use metrics to know your audience and sell subscriptions, why data skills are fundamental to creating equity in newsrooms, how to track team decisions, and thoughtful questions to ask in A/B testing.
